Hello, I am a Senior Lecturer (Associate Professor) at the Computer Science department of the University of Bath, UK. With my PhD students, we are working on exciting problems on sparse and memory/energy efficient neural networks for learning in large output spaces and Large Language Models (LLMs). I am also looking for motivated students to join my group at the University of Bath, UK. Please reach out at rb2608--AT--bath.ac.uk, if you are interested in joining!

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence, Resource-efficient Machine Learning, Learning in large output spaces, AI safety
